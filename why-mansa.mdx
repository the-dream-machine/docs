---
title: Why mansa?
description: "Mansa will be a great fit for your API if any of the following is true:"
---

- You want to make it **effortless** to install, set up and activate your API.
- You want to offer a **great onboarding experience** to developers.
- You want developers to be able to **quickly try out your API** in their own repository without reading the docs.
- You are writing and maintaining CLI tools like `create-myapi-app` or `myapi-init` that **generate boilerplate** for your API.
- You are publishing **example github repositories** that demonstrate how your API works with different js frameworks.
- You are publishing **guides, blog posts and tutorials** to show users how to integrate your API.
